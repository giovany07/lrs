About

Ligon-Razon Solutions is a Philippines-based company that redefines operational support for businesses across the globe. Spearheaded by Mico Joy Ligon and Gene Patrick Razon, we utilize talent for team-building, operations, and business transformation in a unique way.

Throughout the years of working with US and UK-based startups and medium-sized companies, we found a better approach to helping businesses achieve their goals while also uplifting the best of Filipino talent. In our infancy stage, we simply referred skilled workers to trusted clients. However, we quickly learned that clients weren’t only seeking talent, but talent that upholds the values and approach the company is built upon.

As a result of this realization, we kick-started Ligon-Razon Solutions, where referring individuals is just the tip of the iceberg – we also train, oversee, and grow teams according to the unique needs of each business. We also recognize the need to understand your business inside and out, uncover untapped potential, and curate the right teams to elevate your operations to new heights.

Mission

To equip businesses worldwide with professional solutions to their operations while creating remote opportunities for talented individuals to grow and thrive professionally.

Our mission is to create partnerships with businesses worldwide and guide them with the right professional operational solutions to unlock their full business potential.

Vision

To be a highly trusted partner for businesses and their operations worldwide, renowned for building and uplifting a skilled team of professionals to deliver customized operational excellence.

Our vision is to demonstrate that local Filipino ingenuity, work ethic, and heart can be recognized as a solution to growing business opportunities across the globe.
